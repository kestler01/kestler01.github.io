Why Software Engineering

When I was six years old, my dad brought home our first home computer. I distinctly remember this one program called 'CO-CO'. In this program I created my first games by drawing game objects with MS-Paint like simplicity and assigning them variables, action functions and triggers. From this first experience with programming, I was hooked. Software engineering draws on my strengths of creativity, math, and my background in engineering. In many ways I feel like this passion has come full circle, from humble beginnings with 90’s era design games to completing GA boot camp, software engineering has always been a passion. It took me a long time to come back to it, and in the process I’ve had the opportunity to explore other careers in various industries. The accessibility to programs such as GA has allowed me to pursue this passion as my career and am excited to bring everything I have learned along the way to the table.
